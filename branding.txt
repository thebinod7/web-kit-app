## Usage Guidelines

### **Do's**

- Use the gradient for primary brand elements
- Maintain consistent spacing and typography
- Keep designs clean and uncluttered
- Use hover effects for interactivity
- Ensure proper contrast ratios


### **Don'ts**

- Don't use the gradient excessively
- Avoid mixing too many colors
- Don't compromise on readability
- Avoid cluttered layouts
- Don't ignore mobile responsiveness


## Color Combinations

### **Recommended Pairings**

1. **Primary**: Blue gradient + White + Gray-900
2. **Secondary**: Gray-50 + Gray-900 + Blue-600
3. **Accent**: White + Gray-100 + Purple-600
4. **Success**: Green-500 + Green-50 + Gray-900


This branding system creates a cohesive, modern identity perfect for a product discovery platform targeting indie hackers and content creators.
